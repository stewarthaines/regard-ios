Regard for iPad

Have you ever googled for lyrics or chords for a song playing on your iPod?

Regard helps you find web content related to the tunes.

The app saves lyrics/chords/tab that you find and remembers where you found them. Then for other songs it's even simpler than searching. It's more like magic.

It's a work in progress. Pull requests if you'd like to help.
